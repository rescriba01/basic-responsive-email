Designed specifically for working professionals, our 22-month online MPH program enables you to pursue your degree online with minimal time away from your job. Thinking of starting our program next year? Here's a glimpse of what your journey will be like:
  • Starting in August, you will take one course at a time online (about 15-20 hours of work per week) and spend three weeks per year on the Dartmouth campus interacting face-to-face with your classmates and with faculty.
  • Through case studies, problem sets, virtual classes, and video lectures, you will take a deep dive into core MPH content such as epidemiology, biostatistics, and health care ethics.
  • Mentored by a Dartmouth Institute faculty member, you will also design and complete a practicum project. The practicums can range from a quality improvement project in a medical care setting to a project in policy analysis/development.
